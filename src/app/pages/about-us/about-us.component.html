About Us

Welcome to THE LEARNING HUB!

“History will judge us by the difference we make in the everyday lives of Children “ - Nelson Mandela

We started our journey on 14th February 2016 with only twelve learners and gradually the numbers kept on multiplying each year .Our vision and mission is to create Successful life-long Learners with Smiling faces who learn in the real world with confidence, with rightly learned skills, knowledge and compassion in their hearts.

As you will explore our site further ,you will see that we have worked in different modes of learning successfully viz. Traditional schooling ,online platform and also on home schooling . We are committed to providing all our learners with the same innovative Learning curriculum ,no matter where they stay.

We encourage you to read, watch, question, discuss and deeply consider whether joining our enhanced learning system will discover the difference for your family .

Founder & Managing Head of THE LEARNING HUB

Mrs. HEENA UBAID an Edupreneur

I completed my Graduation, B.Ed and CIDTT (Cambridge International Diploma for Teachers and Trainers) from South Mumbai. I have more than fifteen years of experience working in different schools running under different boards and Forms as a Teacher of Pre Primary and Coordinator for Preps. I am highly passionate about Education and believe in Enhancing the living and upliftment of our people through knowledge. The base starts at an early age of a child.
We started THE LEARNING HUB in February 2016 with an aim to provide best quality education to all at an affordable fees, so that none is left behind. When we started we had only 12 learners which kept on multiplying each year. After having worked on Physical schooling, Online mode and on the Homeschooling programme we have realized that Homeschooling works the best with every Learner.
Not all Learners are raised alike and not all learn the same way, Hence we at TLH tap the unseen potential of your child through our Homeschooling programme. In a way, Homeschooling is helping many families by giving the best of education to your child at the comfort of your own home. And also by giving employment to many Experienced and Efficient Teachers who are shaping the future of many.
My Team and I pledge our commitment to your child and your family to assure your association with THE LEARNING HUB Homeschooling will be a rewarding experience to all of us. Welcome to THE LEARNING HUB! (Learning with Difference)
